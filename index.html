What it does

Built for when the network is gone

Every feature here runs without a cell tower, a router, or a subscription.

01

Offline mesh network

Create a resilient communication network without needing cellular data, using Bluetooth LE and local WiFi to reach nearby devices.

02

Encrypted chats

Friend and group conversations are encrypted with a shared key that only your contacts have, so casual snooping on the wire isn't possible.

03

Real-time messaging

Send and receive messages instantly over Bluetooth and WiFi. Automatic message reassembly for large data transfers.

04

Group messaging

Create encrypted group chats with custom group IDs. Support for both friend-to-friend and group conversations.

05

Message acknowledgment

Know when your messages are delivered and read. Built-in acknowledgment system with receipt tracking.

06

No account needed

No phone number, no email, no sign-up. You get a device ID automatically and can start chatting right away.

07

Store & forward relay

If your friend isn't nearby, SOSBlue holds your message on-device and automatically forwards it once a neighboring phone relays it closer — hopping from device to device to extend range far beyond a single Bluetooth link. If any device along the way gets internet access, it can also deliver the message that way, so it arrives however possible.

08

QR code sharing

Quickly add friends by scanning QR codes. Share your user ID and encryption keys securely without manual entry.

09

Hybrid protocol

Uses Bluetooth LE for low-power chat and switches to local WiFi for faster file transfers when devices share the same network.

10

Offline survival bot

A built-in assistant with emergency guidance for situations like fires, floods, and earthquakes — works entirely from a local knowledge base, no internet or signal required.

11

Media gallery in chat

View shared photos and videos right inside the conversation, with a dedicated gallery view for browsing everything a chat or group has shared.

12

Personal file sharing

Pick any files from your device and share them from a local server that nearby devices on the same WiFi network can browse and download, no cables or cloud upload needed.

13

Multilingual smart filter

Built-in content filtering recognizes harmful language across 15 languages, including Bangla, Hindi, Arabic, and more — and catches common tricks like repeated letters or symbol swaps used to slip past filters.

14

Built-in offline notepad

A full text editor baked right into the app — undo/redo, find & replace, and auto-save — so you can jot down notes even with zero connectivity.

15

Self-healing connection

A background watchdog checks on the messaging service and automatically restarts it if the system kills it, so you stay reachable without having to reopen the app.
